Live chat or web chat is a customer service software that’s help business communicate with their visitors. What’s great about this tool, it delivers real time interaction and users can use any hand-held devices or a personal computer to communicate.

Live Chat Helps Generate New Leads

The use of web chat helps gain leads by gathering data such as demographic information, search and purchase history, what information user are viewing, and time of the day customer are viewing the website. The benefit of collecting data draw insight to visitor behavior patterns, that businesses can use to compose a more personalize experience. An establishment of a recommendation box is one way to customize the visitor experience. A recommendation box can help customers narrow their search, down to their preference. This save visitor loads of time because they don’t have to research each and every vehicle. However, achieving this it requires personal information of the visitor lifestyle and personality, or as previously mention, compiling data by how the customer behavior on business website.

The traditional use of a call center involves many call agents handling each individual request via the phone or electronic mail. This method is acceptable; however, it holds some drawback. Given a situation, where a call center will receive loads of incoming calls/emails to the point where it numbers outnumbered the working employees. Creating an unwanted situation of calls pile up and unhappy customers being put on hold. Recruiting more staff probably what comes to mind when counteract this matter. Then again, if the situation were reverse, the business can possibly be losing money because they have to pay their employee even though the call center is lacking visitor calls. This is where live-chat show its favor.
