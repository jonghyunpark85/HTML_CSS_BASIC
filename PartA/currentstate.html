Like any other professional websites, Tesla demonstrates a well-organized site. There is no issue finding information that’s users are looking for. Although this feature is hidden under the support categories, having a search button/key promotes a helpful tool. User has the option to search a question online, promoting a quick and easy answer. It’s crucial to keep an update to Tesla website or any other professional websites. Not having up to date website can cause slow response pages and possibly a broken page.User would rather visit a competitor website than an uncared site. Tesla works on any wide variety of browsers, from Safari of Apple to Chrome on Microsoft. Beside that the website acts and display equality among each browser. With a more hand-held device being used today, Tesla mobile site has been tailor and customize to accommodate user view. All personals details such as customers private information are deemed secure. Making this site safe to purchase.
